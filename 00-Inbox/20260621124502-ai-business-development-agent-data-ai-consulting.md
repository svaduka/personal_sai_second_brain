# AI Business Development Agent for Data & AI Consulting

## Context

- **Origin**: Strategic analysis of agentic product ideas — positioning against crowded "AI sales chatbot" market
- **Date captured**: 2026-06-21
- **Why now**: Generic AI sales chatbots are a crowded market. A specialized agent for Data, AI, Databricks, Snowflake, and Cloud modernization consulting is differentiated and fits directly with professional experience. Combined with the production support agents project, this creates a complete business funnel: AI finds leads → product solves their problem.

## Main Idea

Build a specialized AI business development agent platform that discovers, researches, and engages Data & AI consulting opportunities — not a generic sales chatbot, but a domain-specific opportunity discovery and outreach system targeting Databricks, Snowflake, and cloud modernization signals.

## Architecture — 5 Agent Modules

### Agent 1: Opportunity Discovery Agent
- Scans job postings, RFPs, LinkedIn posts, company announcements, technology migrations, Databricks/Snowflake adoption signals
- Flags patterns like: "Company X is hiring 8 Databricks engineers and 2 architects → likely migration or expansion opportunity"
- Identifies opportunities before competitors

### Agent 2: Research Agent
- Automatically creates company profiles: technology stack, key decision makers, recent initiatives, pain points, competitors
- Builds account intelligence for targeted outreach

### Agent 3: Outreach Agent
- Creates personalized messages based on research
- Example: "Hi John, I noticed your team is expanding Databricks capabilities… We recently helped organizations reduce migration effort by 40%…"
- Tailored to each prospect's specific situation and signals

### Agent 4: Conversation Agent
- Handles early conversations across channels: website chatbot, Teams, email, LinkedIn messaging
- Answers pricing questions, capability questions, solution overviews
- Escalates qualified leads to humans

### Agent 5: Proposal Agent
- Generates solution architecture, scope, timeline, cost estimates, proposal documents
- Templates customized per opportunity type (migration, greenfield, optimization)

## Key Challenges

1. **Getting accurate lead data** — data quality is the hardest part, not the AI
2. **Integrating with email/LinkedIn/CRM systems** — API access, compliance, rate limits
3. **Avoiding spam** — must be genuine and valuable, not mass-blast
4. **Building trust** — prospects must feel the outreach is personalized and relevant

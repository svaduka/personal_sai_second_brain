# Direct Farmer-to-Consumer Platform with Crop Insurance

## Context

- **Origin**: Personal observation — Indian farmers lose 15-40% to middlemen in APMC mandi system
- **Date captured**: 2026-06-21
- **Why now**: India has 86% small/marginal farmers (< 2 hectares) who bear all risk but capture smallest value share. Digital infrastructure (UPI, Aadhaar, smartphone penetration) now makes direct-to-consumer feasible at scale.

## Main Idea

Build a platform that eliminates intermediaries between Indian farmers and consumers, with built-in micro crop insurance funded through the platform, and a distribution network that spreads customer demand across farmers to ensure stable income and growth.

## Three Pillars

### Pillar 1: Direct Farmer-to-Consumer Marketplace
- Farmers list produce directly on the platform
- Consumers (urban households, restaurants, hotels) buy fresh from source
- Platform handles logistics and quality assurance
- Eliminates 4-6 intermediaries in current supply chain

### Pillar 2: Micro Crop Insurance
- Insurance built into every transaction (small % surcharge or platform-funded from commission)
- Protects farmers against crop failure, weather damage, pest damage
- Accessible to small/marginal farmers who can't access traditional crop insurance
- Could partner with existing insurers or build a pooled risk model

### Pillar 3: Distribution & Demand Spreading
- Spread customer base across regions to ensure consistent demand
- Reduce farmer dependency on single buyer or local mandi
- Subscription/recurring orders for predictable farmer income
- Cooperative model where farmers pool supply for larger orders

## Open Questions (To Explore)

- Target consumer: Urban households? Restaurants/hotels/canteens? Both?
- Insurance model: Consumer surcharge, platform commission, or hybrid?
- Logistics: Platform fleet, third-party (Dunzo, Porter), or farmer cooperatives?
- Geography: Start with one state/region or national?
- Revenue model: Transaction fee? Subscription? Premium tiers?
- Differentiation from Ninjacart, DeHaat, AgroStar, BigBasket farm program?
- Regulatory: APMC Act reforms, e-NAM integration, FSSAI compliance?
- Cold chain: How to handle perishables at scale?
